Lazy Pagination Demo

How Lazy Pagination Works:

  • Initial Load: Page loads instantly with first 25 models
  • Load More: Click "Load More" to fetch additional models via AJAX
  • No Page Reload: New content appends seamlessly without full page refresh
  • Preserves Filters: All current filters are maintained across pagination
  • Performance: Dramatically faster than loading all models at once
